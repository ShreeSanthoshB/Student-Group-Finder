About Study Group Finder

Who We Are

Study Group Finder is an academic collaboration platform that helps students connect with classmates who share the same learning goals. It makes studying more interactive by bringing students together in one place.


Our Mission

Our mission is to encourage collaborative learning by making it simple to create study groups, schedule sessions, share notes, and support one another throughout the academic journey.
